About Me

I am M. Robert Rajiah

Assistant Professor • Graphic Designer

Currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Visual Communication at Thanthai Hans Roever College, Perambalur.

I am a passionate Graphic Designer with a Master's degree in Visual Communication, blending academic depth with creative industry expertise. My journey focuses on creating visually engaging solutions that bridge the gap between abstract concepts and effective communication.

With high proficiency in professional tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, I empower designs with technical precision and artistic flair to deliver results with a distinct professional edge.
